Fill in the form below and we'll send you a dedicated club link your players can use — no setup required on your end.

What happens next:
  1. We create your club sandbox in BoxLeague Pro
  2. You get an email within 24 hours with a dedicated link (e.g. ?club=your-club)
  3. Share that link with your players — they land straight in your club, no setup
  4. You can add players, create leagues, and manage availability from the club organiser tools
Tell us about your club in the Message field:
  • Club name & location (e.g. "East Grinstead Tennis Club, West Sussex")
  • Sport (tennis / padel / squash / racketball / badminton)
  • How many leagues you want to run to start with
  • Court booking system URL if you have one (ClubSpark, Playtomic, etc.)
  • Anything else we should know
