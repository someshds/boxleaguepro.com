Two Ways to Play
Competitive Leagues or Friendly Matches

Whether you run a structured box league or just want to find players for a hit, BoxLeague Pro has you covered.

🏆
Structured
Box Leagues

Organised rounds with teams, availability matching, and automatic scheduling. Singles or doubles.

  • Self-service onboarding — no admin bottleneck
  • 6 daily time slots across a rolling calendar
  • Match Finder finds times when everyone's free
  • One-tap booking with instant notifications
  • Progress tracking and deadline reminders
  • WhatsApp share to chase missing players
🎉
Open
Mix-In Games

Post a game, set the date and time, and let other players join. No league required — just turn up and play.

  • Create games for any sport and skill level
  • Choose Fun, Intermediate, or Advanced
  • Share game links on WhatsApp with one tap
  • See all upcoming games with availability
  • No league membership needed — any player can join
  • Link to your club's court booking system
Packed with Features
Everything You Need to Run Your League

Built by a player, for players. Every feature solves a real problem.

📅

Smart Availability

Mark your free slots across 6 daily time windows. Copy last week, clear the week, or update slot by slot.

✅

Match Finder

Automatically finds time slots when all players in a match are available. One tap to book.

👥

Everyone View

Colour-coded calendar showing who's free and when. Chase missing players via WhatsApp.

🎉

Open Matches

Post games for any sport and level. Other players join until spots fill up. Share links instantly.

Club & League System

Create your club, add leagues within it, share codes. Self-service — no single organiser bottleneck.

Court Booking Link

League Chat

Real-time messaging for your league. Coordinate, banter, and stay connected between matches.

Player Profiles

WhatsApp Sharing

Share leagues, matches, and open games with pre-formatted WhatsApp messages. One tap.
